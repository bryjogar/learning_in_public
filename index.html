About Me

I live on 3 acres in northeast Texas with my wife and four kids. By day, I serve churches and non-profits nationwide as a Solutions Architect for an MSP. I primarily focus on network infrastructure (Ruckus/SonicWall) and the Microsoft 365 ecosystem.

Academically, I hold degrees in Biblical Studies and Apologetics from Liberty University. I have a deep love for comparative religion and the intersection of ancient faith and modern life.

I use Obsidian to manage my knowledge attempting to use the Zettelkasten method. This site is an attempt to "learn in public" and share the connections I make between technology, theology, and family life.
